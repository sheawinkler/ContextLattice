ContextLattice — Private, reusable context for AI agents

ContextLattice reduces long‑context costs and failures by turning context into reusable memory. Local‑first, private by default, and enterprise‑ready.

What it does

  • Stores agent context once and retrieves it when needed
  • Reduces long‑context token spend and truncation failures
  • Runs privately (self‑hosted) or as a managed service

Who it’s for

  • LLM platform teams and AI infra teams
  • IDE agents and vibecoder workflows
  • Enterprise teams with private data

Pilot (2–4 weeks)

  • After metrics + ROI summary

Why now
